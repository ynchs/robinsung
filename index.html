In 2023, I was handed a program that had been failing for years. For over a decade, KOTRA's overseas tradeshow program followed the same playbook: rent a $70K booth, send cold emails, and hope someone would stop by (They rarely did). There was no follow-up, no measurable return. By 2025, the Korean government was preparing to shut the conference programs down.

In one year, I rebuilt the program and achieved what it hadn't in ten: the first trackable ROI from partner deals with U.S. enterprises, and a government decision to continue the program. I established a new standard for what partner marketing could look like at KOTRA Silicon Valley.
